Data security is the process of safeguarding digital information throughout its entire life cycle to protect it from corruption, theft, or unauthorised access. It covers everything - hardware, software, storage devices, and user devices; access and administrative controls; and organizations’ policies and procedures. Digital citizenship emphasises individual responsibility to participate in society. It is the attributes that empower by emphasising citizens’ abilities (and responsibilities) to understand, navigate and participate in the digital world.
